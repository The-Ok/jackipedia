JackSucksAtLife (channel)

JackSucksAtLife or @JackSucksAtLife is the main channel of Jack's. Originally created on March 10th, 2013 under the original name JackSucksAtMinecraft, @JackSucksAtLife started as a a gaming channel focusing on the hit video game, Minecraft. @JackSucksAtLife now does videos relating to YouTube and other stuff, mainly fitness(now on @Jakmw). The most viewed video on @JackSucksAtLife is "This is the LONGEST Name on a Youtube Play Button EVER!" with ~17 million views as of March 2026.
